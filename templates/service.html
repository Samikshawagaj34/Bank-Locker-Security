Our Services

Bank Locker

Banks offer locker facilities to individuals as required. Both public and private sector banks provide this service for a small annual fee. These lockers are maintained in a secure facility under constant surveillance and security. There are several advantages to availing a bank locker.

Face Detection

Face detection is a technology used to identify and locate human faces in images or videos. It serves as a foundational step in many applications, including facial recognition, emotion detection, and video surveillance.

OTP Authentication

OTP (One-Time Password) Authentication is a security mechanism that generates a unique password for a single use, enhancing the security of user accounts and transactions.
